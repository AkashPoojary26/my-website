KAMBALA

The famous buffalo race held in slushy fields — a symbol of Tulunadu’s power and pride.

BHOOTA KOLA

A sacred ritual celebrating divine spirits (Daivas) with dance, devotion and tradition.

KEDDASA

The Mother Earth worship festival marking agricultural renewal and gratitude.

NAGARADHANE

The sacred serpent worship of Tulunadu performed in Nagabanas, symbolizing protection, fertility and the bond with nature.

TEMPLE CHARIOT FESTIVAL

Rathotsava — the majestic pulling of the huge temple chariot by devotees.
